Publications

This page contains my research publications in computational geometry.

City Guarding with Cameras of Bounded Field of View (Journal Version)

Authors: A. Biniaz, M. Hashemi
Venue: Journal of Computational Geometry: Theory and Applications
Year: 2025

An extended journal version of the city guarding problem, providing tight bounds and generalizations for guarding free space among buildings and convex shapes using cameras with limited field of view.

Guarding Polygons With Mutually Visible π-Guards

Authors: A. Ahadi, A. Biniaz, M. Hashemi, A. Nakhaeisharif
Venue: 37th Canadian Conference on Computational Geometry (CCCG)
Year: 2025

We study polygon guarding with 180° vertex guards under mutual visibility constraints and establish upper and lower bounds on the number of guards required for general and orthogonal polygons.

City Guarding with Cameras of Bounded Field of View

Authors: A. Biniaz, M. Hashemi
Venue: 35th Canadian Conference on Computational Geometry (CCCG)
Year: 2023

We study variants of the city guarding and art gallery problems and prove tight bounds on the number of 180° field-of-view cameras required to guard free space among rectangular buildings and orthogonally convex polygons.
